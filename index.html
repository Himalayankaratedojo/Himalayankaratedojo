Welcome To Our Karate Dojo

We are dedicated to fostering physical fitness, mental discipline, and self-defense skills through a diverse range of Karate styles.

Himalayan Karate Dojo is heaven for martial arts enthusiasts located in the heart of Kathmandu, Nepal. Established in 2018 AD, our dojo at Kumarigal, Chabahil Ward No. 7. Our Dojo stands as a testament to the rich tradition of Karate in the region providing a welcoming environment for practitioners of all levels to embark on a journey of personal growth and martial arts excellence.

Consistent Practice
